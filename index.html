Methodology

The board of education adopted is CBSE, following the syllabus prescribed by NCERT.

Methodology - XSEED CBSE

XSEED CBSE is a child carbric world clam methodology incorporating the 21st century education skills, of critical thinking, problem solving, collaboration, communication and global citizenship. It is an inquiry based constructivist approach that uses hands-on-activities followed by reflection and feedback, resulting in Academic independence and competence.

Culmination Events / XSEED DAY

Events are organized every term to exhibit the skill and concept learnt over the term in the core subjects and languages with parent - audience.
Parents come to know directly about the concepts/skills acquired by their wards. It also ensures timely and periodic intervention by parents if required.

Sciencify for everyday science

This weekly science activity ensures understanding the science facts, science in daily life justifying education for life.
